Pet Sitting

This is a service where a person comes over to your home to care for your pet while you are away, such as on vacation or business travel. It is the opposite of taking your pet to a boarding facility to be cared for exclusively at that location.
Charges are KShs. 3,000 per hour.

Pet Walking

This is the act of a person walking with a pet, typically from the pet's residence and then returning. Leashes are commonly used for this especially for dogs. Pets receive many benefits, including exercise and companionship.
Charges are KShs. 3,000 per hour.

Pet Boarding

This is sending your pet to our facility for our team to watch over your pet.
Boarding means a place your pet stays overnight. Usually people choose to board their pets when they travel.
Charges are KShs. 6,000 per night.

Pet Grooming

This is the grooming of a domestic animal, including bathing, cutting of hair,
trimming of nails,
and other services generally associated with the act of grooming.
Charges are KShs. 5,000 per session.
